# **App Name**: LexDigitalis

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Displays a title, subtitle, and contact button for initial engagement.
- Legal Services Section: Organizes legal services into distinct cards (e.g., labor, civil, criminal, commercial).
- Professional Team Section: Presents team members with photos and specializations.
- Achievements Section: Highlights key achievements or experiences of the firm.
- Client Testimonials: Displays client testimonials to build trust.
- Contact Form & Information: Provides a contact form and essential contact details (WhatsApp, address, email, map).
- AI Legal Insights Summarizer: Utilizes AI tool to summarize complex legal documents, making key information accessible. Users can upload legal text or link to online resources, and the AI tool generates a concise summary, identifying essential points and relevant clauses to provide clients and internal teams with quick and informed insights.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Metallic gold/mustard (#D4AF37) for buttons and accents to convey premium service.
- Background color: Very dark gray, almost black (#1A1A1A) to set a serious and trustworthy tone.
- Accent color: A slightly darker gold (#C5A028) for hover interactions. Sublte and sophisticated. Do not overuse the color
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif. High authority feel.
- Body font: 'Inter', sans-serif. To increase modernity and legibility.
- Use simple, professional icons related to legal services. Keep it minimal.
- Layout: Card-based design with a clean, professional look.
